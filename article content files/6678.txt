Looking at the universe of stocks we cover at Dividend Channel, on 3/7/23, Edgewell Personal Care Co (Symbol: EPC), Texas Roadhouse Inc (Symbol: TXRH), and Monro Inc (Symbol: MNRO) will all trade ex-dividend for their respective upcoming dividends. As a percentage of EPC's recent stock price of $43.42, this dividend works out to approximately 0.35%, so look for shares of Edgewell Personal Care Co to trade 0.35% lower — all else being equal — when EPC shares open for trading on 3/7/23.
