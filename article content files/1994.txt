Looking at the universe of stocks we cover at Dividend Channel, on 3/16/23, Silgan Holdings Inc (Symbol: SLGN), Methanex Corp (Symbol: MEOH), and Albemarle Corp. (Symbol: ALB) will all trade ex-dividend for their respective upcoming dividends.  Silgan Holdings Inc will pay its quarterly dividend of $0.18 on 3/31/23, Methanex Corp will pay its quarterly dividend of $0.175 on 3/31/23, and Albemarle Corp. will pay its quarterly dividend of $0.40 on 4/3/23.  

As a percentage of SLGN's recent stock price of $51.98, this dividend works out to approximately 0.35%, so look for shares of Silgan Holdings Inc to trade 0.35% lower — all else being equal — when SLGN shares open for trading on 3/16/23.  Similarly, investors should look for MEOH to open 0.36% lower in price and for ALB to open 0.18% lower, all else being equal.
