In trading on Wednesday, shares of Voya Financial Inc's 5.35% Fixed-Rate Reset Non-Cumulative Preferred Stock, Series B (Symbol: VOYA.PRB) were yielding above the 6% mark based on its quarterly dividend (annualized to $1.3375), with shares changing hands as low as $22.23 on the day.    This compares to an average yield of 6.60% in the "Financial" preferred stock category, according to Preferred Stock Channel.    As of last close, VOYA.PRB was trading at a 8.92% discount to its liquidation preference amount, versus the average discount of 12.15% in the "Financial" category.   Investors should keep in mind that the shares are not cumulative, meaning that in the event of a missed payment, the company does not have to pay the balance of missed dividends to preferred shareholders before resuming a common dividend.
