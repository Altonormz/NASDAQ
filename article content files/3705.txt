On 3/14/23, Public Storage's 6.5% Cumulative Preferred Share of Beneficial Interest, Series P (Symbol: PSA.PRP) will trade ex-dividend, for its quarterly dividend of $0.25, payable on 3/30/23.  As a percentage of PSA.PRP's recent share price of $17.57, this dividend works out to approximately 1.42%, so look for shares of PSA.PRP to trade 1.42% lower — all else being equal — when PSA.PRP shares open for trading on 3/14/23. On an annualized basis, the current yield is approximately 5.62%, which compares to an average yield of 7.73% in the "Real Estate" preferred stock category, according to Preferred Stock Channel.
