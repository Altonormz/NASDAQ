Looking at units outstanding versus one week prior within the universe of ETFs covered at ETF Channel, the biggest outflow was seen in the UNG ETF, where 19,400,000 units were destroyed, or a 13.0% decrease week over week.  



And on a percentage change basis, the ETF with the biggest outflow was the SPDR S&P Retail ETF, which lost 2,250,000 of its units, representing a 35.4% decline in outstanding units compared to the week prior. Among the largest underlying components of XRT, in morning trading today Carvana is up about 1.8%, and Sally Beauty Holdings is lower by about 1.3%.
