China is about to undergo its biggest economic policymaker shuffle in decades — and Western investors are watching closely. The National People's Congress — or NPC — kicks off its annual session in Beijing on March 5th. It’s there that China will set a new budget and mark the end of a long line of pro-market reformers in control of the economy.
