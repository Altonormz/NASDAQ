In trading on Tuesday, precious metals shares were relative laggards, down on the day by about 3.6%.  Helping drag down the group were shares of McEwen Mining, down about 8.6% and shares of Centerra Gold off about 6.2% on the day.



Also lagging the market Tuesday are metals & mining shares, down on the day by about 2.1% as a group, led down by Atlas Lithium, trading lower by about 7.8% and Largo, trading lower by about 6%.
