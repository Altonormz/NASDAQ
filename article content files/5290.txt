In trading on Tuesday, shares of Air Lease Corp's 6.150% Fixed-to-Floating Rate Non-Cumulative Perpetual Preferred Stock, Series A  (Symbol: AL.PRA) were yielding above the 6.5% mark based on its quarterly dividend (annualized to $1.5375), with shares changing hands as low as $23.53 on the day.    This compares to an average yield of 8.23% in the "Transportation" preferred stock category, according to Preferred Stock Channel.    As of last close, AL.PRA was trading at a 2.20% discount to its liquidation preference amount, versus the average premium of 0.02% in the "Transportation" category.   Investors should keep in mind that the shares are not cumulative, meaning that in the event of a missed payment, the company does not have to pay the balance of missed dividends to preferred shareholders before resuming a common dividend.
