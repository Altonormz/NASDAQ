(RTTNews) - Verisk Analytics Inc. (VRSK) reported revenue from continuing operations of $630.4 million for the fourth quarter, nearly flat with $630.3 million last year.
Earnings from continuing operations for the quarter increased to $215.8 million or $1.37 a share from $118.3 million or $0.73 a share last year.
Adjusted earnings from continuing operations were reported at $1.43  per share compared with $1.21 a year ago. 
According to the company, "The Energy business qualified as held for sale and discontinued operations in the fourth quarter of 2022. Accordingly, all results of the Energy business have been removed from continuing operations."
