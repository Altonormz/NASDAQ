In early trading on Thursday, shares of American Express topped the list of the day's best performing Dow Jones Industrial Average components, trading up 2.0%.  Year to date, American Express registers a 20.7% gain.

Two other components making moves today are Johnson & Johnson, trading down 0.6%, and Intel, trading up 1.5% on the day.
