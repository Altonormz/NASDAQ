Looking at the universe of stocks we cover at Dividend Channel, on 3/9/23, Columbia Sportswear Co. (Symbol: COLM), Kimberly-Clark Corp. (Symbol: KMB), and VF Corp. (Symbol: VFC) will all trade ex-dividend for their respective upcoming dividends.  Columbia Sportswear Co. will pay its quarterly dividend of $0.30 on 3/21/23, Kimberly-Clark Corp. will pay its quarterly dividend of $1.18 on 4/4/23, and VF Corp. will pay its quarterly dividend of $0.30 on 3/21/23.  

As a percentage of COLM's recent stock price of $88.44, this dividend works out to approximately 0.34%, so look for shares of Columbia Sportswear Co. to trade 0.34% lower — all else being equal — when COLM shares open for trading on 3/9/23.  Similarly, investors should look for KMB to open 0.93% lower in price and for VFC to open 1.20% lower, all else being equal.
