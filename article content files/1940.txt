On 3/16/23, Gladstone Commercial Corp's 7.50% Series B Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock (Symbol: GOODO) will trade ex-dividend, for its monthly dividend of $0.125, payable on 3/31/23.  As a percentage of GOODO's recent share price of $17.99, this dividend works out to approximately 0.69%, so look for shares of GOODO to trade 0.69% lower — all else being equal — when GOODO shares open for trading on 3/16/23. On an annualized basis, the current yield is approximately 8.98%, which compares to an average yield of 8.02% in the "Real Estate" preferred stock category, according to Preferred Stock Channel.
