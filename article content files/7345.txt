(RTTNews) - Silvergate Capital Corp. (SI) shares are sliding more than 41 percent on Thursday morning trade after the company said there will be a delay in its annual reports. The company was planning to publish its Annual reports on March 16. 
The shares have been on a decline since mid-February. 
Currently, shares are at $7.66, down 43.40 percent from the previous close of $13.53 on a volume of 23,877,233.
