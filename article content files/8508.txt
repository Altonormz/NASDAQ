Looking at the universe of stocks we cover at Dividend Channel, on 3/2/23, Genuine Parts Co. (Symbol: GPC), PepsiCo Inc (Symbol: PEP), and General Motors Co (Symbol: GM) will all trade ex-dividend for their respective upcoming dividends.  Genuine Parts Co. will pay its quarterly dividend of $0.95 on 4/3/23, PepsiCo Inc will pay its quarterly dividend of $1.15 on 3/31/23, and General Motors Co will pay its quarterly dividend of $0.09 on 3/16/23.  

As a percentage of GPC's recent stock price of $178.25, this dividend works out to approximately 0.53%, so look for shares of Genuine Parts Co. to trade 0.53% lower — all else being equal — when GPC shares open for trading on 3/2/23.  Similarly, investors should look for PEP to open 0.66% lower in price and for GM to open 0.23% lower, all else being equal.
