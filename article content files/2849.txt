In early trading on Monday, shares of Amgen topped the list of the day's best performing Dow Jones Industrial Average components, trading up 2.9%.  Year to date, Amgen Inc has lost about 10.7% of its value.



And the worst performing Dow component thus far on the day is American Express, trading down 3.5%.  American Express is showing a gain of 8.2% looking at the year to date performance.



Two other components making moves today are Walt Disney, trading down 2.8%, and Procter & Gamble, trading up 1.9% on the day.
