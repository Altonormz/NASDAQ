WASHINGTON, March 7 (Reuters) - The U.S. Justice Department filed a complaint against JetBlue JBLU.O in Boston federal court on Tuesday amid expectation that the government will oppose the airline's purchase of Spirit SAVE.N.
The complaint was not immediately available.
