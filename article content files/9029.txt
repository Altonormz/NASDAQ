In early trading on Monday, shares of Boeing topped the list of the day's best performing Dow Jones Industrial Average components, trading up 1.4%.  Year to date, Boeing registers a 5.5% gain.

Two other components making moves today are Merck, trading down 0.2%, and Caterpillar, trading up 1.3% on the day.
