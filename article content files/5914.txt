Looking at units outstanding versus one week prior within the universe of ETFs covered at ETF Channel, the biggest outflow was seen in the Vanguard Intermediate-Term Treasury ETF, where 14,541,437 units were destroyed, or a 6.2% decrease week over week.  



And on a percentage change basis, the ETF with the biggest outflow was the BMAR ETF, which lost 1,000,000 of its units, representing a 31.0% decline in outstanding units compared to the week prior.
