In early trading on Tuesday, shares of American Express topped the list of the day's best performing Dow Jones Industrial Average components, trading up 0.7%.  Year to date, American Express registers a 18.1% gain.

Two other components making moves today are Merck, trading down 1.0%, and JPMorgan Chase, trading up 0.5% on the day.
