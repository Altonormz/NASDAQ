Viatris Inc (Symbol: VTRS) has been named as a Top 10 dividend stock, according the most recent Dividend Channel ''DividendRank'' report.   The report noted that among the coverage universe, VTRS shares displayed both attractive valuation metrics and strong profitability metrics.  For example, the recent VTRS share price of $10.56 represents a price-to-book ratio of 0.6 and an annual dividend yield of 4.55% — by comparison, the average company in Dividend Channel's coverage universe yields 4.0% and trades at a price-to-book ratio of 2.5.  The report also cited the strong quarterly dividend history at Viatris Inc, and favorable long-term multi-year growth rates in key fundamental data points.



The report stated, ''Dividend investors approaching investing from a value standpoint are generally most interested in researching the strongest most profitable companies, that also happen to be trading at an attractive valuation.  That's what we aim to find using our proprietary DividendRank formula, which ranks the coverage universe based upon our various criteria for both profitability and valuation, to generate a list of the top most 'interesting' stocks, meant for investors as a source of ideas that merit further research.''


The annualized dividend paid by Viatris Inc is $0.48/share, currently paid in quarterly installments, and its most recent dividend ex-date was on 03/08/2023.
