In trading on Monday, shares of Citigroup Inc's 6.875% Fixed Rate/Floating Rate Noncumulative Preferred Stock, Series K (Symbol: C.PRK) were yielding above the 7% mark based on its quarterly dividend (annualized to $1.7188), with shares changing hands as low as $24.45 on the day.    This compares to an average yield of 6.41% in the "Financial" preferred stock category, according to Preferred Stock Channel.    As of last close, C.PRK was trading at a 0.40% discount to its liquidation preference amount, versus the average discount of 9.94% in the "Financial" category.   Investors should keep in mind that the shares are not cumulative, meaning that in the event of a missed payment, the company does not have to pay the balance of missed dividends to preferred shareholders before resuming a common dividend.
